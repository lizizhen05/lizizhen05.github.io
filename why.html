Why I Love Them

I like visual novels because they create a special space between reading and playing. They are quiet, detailed, and often more focused on emotion than many other kinds of games.

I also enjoy how visual novels build strong characters. Because the stories move through dialogue, thoughts, and repeated choices, players often get to know characters in a deeper way. Even small moments can become very memorable.

Another reason I love them is atmosphere. Music, color, background art, and pacing all shape the experience. A visual novel can feel relaxing, mysterious, heartbreaking, or intense without needing complicated gameplay.

For me, visual novels show that games can be literary, emotional, and personal at the same time.
