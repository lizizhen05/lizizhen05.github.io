My Visual Novel Project

I am also interested in creating a visual novel of my own. What attracts me most is the way this genre allows story, mood, and choice to work together.

If I create one, I would want it to focus on character relationships, emotional tension, and different story routes. I am especially interested in how player choices can slowly change the meaning of a scene or the direction of a relationship.

I also like the visual side of the genre. Backgrounds, color, text, and music can all shape the feeling of a story. Because of that, making a visual novel feels like combining writing, design, and interactive storytelling into one project.

Right now, this is still a project in progress, but it is one of the reasons I care so much about visual novels in the first place.
