What Are Visual Novels?

Visual novels are story-driven games that combine reading with images, music, dialogue, and sometimes player choices. Instead of focusing on fast action, they usually focus on characters, mood, and narrative.

Many visual novels include background art, character sprites, soundtracks, and branching paths. Some have multiple endings based on the player’s decisions. Others are more linear, but still create a strong feeling of immersion through writing and atmosphere.

What makes visual novels special is the way they turn reading into an interactive experience. Players do not simply watch events happen. They move through the story, make choices, and build emotional connections with characters over time.
