Visual novels combine text, artwork, music, and player choice. Some focus on mystery, romance, or fantasy, while others become emotional, surreal, or experimental. This site introduces the genre, shares some of my favorite titles, and includes a page about Riverse, a visual novel project I hope to create.

“Some stories flow forward. Others return.”
