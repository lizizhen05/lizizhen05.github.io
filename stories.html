Stories You Can Read and Play

An introduction to visual novels, favorite titles, and a project in progress

Visual novels are story-based games that combine text, artwork, music, and player choice. Some focus on mystery, romance, or fantasy, while others tell emotional and experimental stories. This site introduces the genre, shares some of my favorite titles, and includes a page about a visual novel I hope to create.

“Some stories are not only read, but played.”
