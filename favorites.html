Favorite Titles

  • Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney – A dramatic and memorable series that combines mystery, humor, and strong character writing.
  • Doki Doki Literature Club – A game that begins like a simple school story but becomes much darker and more experimental.
  • Steins;Gate – A science fiction visual novel known for its emotional story and complex ideas about time and choice.
  • The House in Fata Morgana – A gothic and emotionally powerful story with a striking atmosphere and layered narrative.
  • Coffee Talk – A quieter interactive story game that uses conversation, music, and mood to create a comforting experience.

I enjoy titles that combine strong narrative with a clear emotional tone. Some are suspenseful, some are experimental, and some are comforting, but all of them show how flexible visual novels can be as a form.
