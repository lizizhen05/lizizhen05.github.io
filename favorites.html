Favorite Titles

  • Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney Trilogy – One of my favorite examples of character-driven mystery and dramatic storytelling.
  • STEINS;GATE – A memorable visual novel about time, consequence, and emotional attachment.
  • AI: The Somnium Files – A strong mix of investigation, surreal imagery, and branching narrative design.
  • Raging Loop – A story that uses repetition, suspense, and looping structure in a way I find especially compelling.
  • Slay the Princess – A striking example of how voice, repetition, and choice can transform a story.

These are not only games I enjoy, but also titles that shape the way I think about interactive storytelling. I am especially drawn to works that use mystery, repetition, emotional tension, and route structure in creative ways.
