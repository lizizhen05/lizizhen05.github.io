Where to Start

For beginners, it helps to start with visual novels or interactive story games that are easy to follow and emotionally engaging.

  • Ace Attorney – Good for players who like mystery, humor, and dramatic characters.
  • Coffee Talk – A calm and accessible choice for players who want a slower and more relaxing experience.
  • Doki Doki Literature Club – Best for players who are interested in unusual storytelling, but it becomes dark quickly.
  • Our Life – A gentle and character-focused story that is easy for new players to enjoy.

These titles show that visual novels can be suspenseful, emotional, experimental, or comforting. They are a good starting point for discovering what kind of stories you enjoy most.
